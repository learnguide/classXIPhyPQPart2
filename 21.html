25.Why is a soap solution a better cleansing agent than ordinary water?
Ans.
Since a cloth has narrow spaces in the form of fine capillaries, Capillary rise is given
by:
→
h = height of capillary
T = Tension surface
Θ = Angle of contact
r = Radius
P = Density
g = Acceleration due to gravity.
Now, addition of soap to water reduces the angle of contact θ, this will increase Cosθ and
hence the value of h. that is, the soap water will rise more in narrow spaces in the cloth and
clean fabrics better than water alone.
