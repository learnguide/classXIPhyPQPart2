33.Why does the cloud seem floating in the sky?
Ans.
The terminal velocity of a raindrop is directly proportional to the square of radius of
drop. When falling, large drops have high terminal velocities while small drops have small
terminal velocities hence the small drops falls so slowly that cloud seems floating.
