The period of swing of simple pendulum will remain unchanged till the location of
centre of gravity of the bob left after melting of the ice remains at the fixed position from the
point of suspension. If centre of gravity of ice bob after melting is raised upwards, then
effective length of pendulum decreases and hence time period of swing decreases. Similarly,
if centre of gravity shifts downward, time period increases.
