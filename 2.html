6.A drop of oil placed on the surface of water spreads out, but a drop of water placed on
oil contracts. Why?
Ans .
Since the cohesive forces between the oil molecules are less than the adhesive force
between the oil molecules and the drop of oil spreads out and reverse holds for drop of
water.

8.The diameter of ball A is half that of ball B. What will be their ratio of their terminal
velocities in water?
Ans.
The terminal velocity is directly proportional to the square of radius of the ball,
therefore the ratio of terminal velocities will be 1:4.
