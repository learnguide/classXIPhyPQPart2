30.What is terminal velocity? What is the terminal velocity of a body in a freely falling
system?
Ans.
It is the maximum constant velocity acquired by the body while falling freely in a
viscous medium. In a freely falling system, g = 0. Therefore, the terminal velocity of the body
will also be zero.
31.What is the cause of viscosity in a fluid? How does the flow of fluid depend on
viscosity?
Ans.
Internal friction is the cause of viscosity of fluid. The flow of fluid decreases when
viscosity increases, because viscosity is a frictional force and greater the friction, lesser is the
flow of liquid.
