(c)
The particle is executing a simple harmonic motion. O is the mean position of the particle. Its
velocity at the mean position O is the maximum. The value for velocity is negative as the
particle is directed leftward. The acceleration and force of a particle executing SHM is zero at
the mean position.
(d)
The particle is moving toward point O from the end B. This direction of motion is opposite to
the conventional positive direction, which is from A to B. Hence, the particle's velocity and
acceleration, and the force on it are all negative.
(e)
The particle is moving toward point O from the end A. This direction of motion is from A to
B, which is the conventional positive direction. Hence, the values for velocity, acceleration,
and force are all positive.
