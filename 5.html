16.The accumulation of snow on an aero plane wing may reduce the lift. Explain?
Ans .
Due to the accumulation of snow on the wings of the aero plane, the structure of wings
no larger remains as that of aerofoil. As a result, the net upward force (i.e. lift) is decreased.
17.The antiseptics used for cuts and wounds in human flesh have low surface tension.
Why?
Ans.
Since the surface tension of antiseptics is less, they spread more on cuts and wounds and
as a result, cut or wound is healed quickly.
